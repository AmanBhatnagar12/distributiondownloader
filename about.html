Distribution Downloader 1.0.0 Alpha

Distribution Downloader is a small graphical application developed in UNIX Shell/Bash, using Zenity and WGet, and designed for Linux newcomers who want a neat tool for downloading various distributions.
At the moment, the app supports some of the most important distributions of GNU/Linux, including Ubuntu, Arch Linux, CentOS, Fedora, Linux Mint, openSUSE, Slackware, Puppy Linux, and Manjaro.

Requirements:

  • Zenity
  • Wget
